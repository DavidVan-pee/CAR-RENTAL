About Us

Vroom Services is a car rental company located in East Legon. We provide services to people living close there and it's environs. The address is P.0 Box 712, Oblogo Road opposite the Goil filling station.

We deal with all kinds of cars ranging from normal commercial cars such as the Toyota Yarris to high end luxury cars such as the Lamborghini Aventador. Our prices are fixed as seen on the website and non-negotiable. Discount offers can be given to customers during special seasons like Christmas or some holidays
